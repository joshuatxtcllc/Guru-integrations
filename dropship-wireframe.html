Wireframes for dropshipping integration with Frame Destination and Frame It Easy

Customer Experience - No Visible Change
Customer Order Process
The customer experience remains unchanged. Customers will not know if their order is fulfilled in-house or by a vendor partner.
Standard Frame Order Process
  1. Customer selects frame style, size, and options on Frame Guru website
  2. Customer uploads artwork or selects to ship artwork separately
  3. Customer completes checkout and receives order confirmation
  4. Customer receives Frame Guru branded shipping confirmation
  5. Customer receives Frame Guru branded packaging (drop-shipped by vendor)
